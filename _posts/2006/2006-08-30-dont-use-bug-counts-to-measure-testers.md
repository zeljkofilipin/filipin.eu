title: Don’t Use Bug Counts to Measure Testers
---
I have just read a short article, [Don’t Use Bug Counts to Measure Testers](http://www.kaner.com/pdfs/bugcount.pdf) by [Cem Kaner](http://www.kaner.com/).

The last paragraph:

> If you really need a simple number to use to rank your testers, use a random number generator. It is fairer than bug counting, it probably creates less political infighting, and it might be more accurate.

[Brian Marick](http://www.testing.com/cgi-bin/blog) mentioned it at [agile-testing@yahoogroups.com](http://groups.yahoo.com/group/agile-testing/).
