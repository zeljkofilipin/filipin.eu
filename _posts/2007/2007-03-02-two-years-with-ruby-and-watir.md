According to [Watir Users](http://wiki.openqa.org/display/WTR/Watir+Users) page at Watir wiki I have been using [Ruby](http://www.ruby-lang.org/en/) and [Watir](http://www.openqa.org/watir/) for two years. I checked [SVN](http://subversion.tigris.org/) log, and the first entry is dated 11th March 2005. I checked out the code. There is 58 files in three folders. Tests are for two projects, and the biggest file has over 600 lines, so I guess I was writing scripts for month or two before I checked it into SVN.

By the way, the code is horrible. :) I have read somewhere that if you look at your old code and you do not think

> What was I thinking when I wrote this?!?!

it means that you have not learned anything since then.

It has been interesting two years.
