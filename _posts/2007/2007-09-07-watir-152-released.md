I came back from a long vacation, and what a surprise. Watir 1.5.2 is out!

According to RubyForge, it was [over two years since the last major update](http://rubyforge.org/frs/?group_id=104) (1.4.1). There were [a lot of minor updates](http://wiki.openqa.org/display/WTR/Development+Builds) in the meantime, and you could always [get the latest source](http://wiki.openqa.org/display/WTR/FAQ#FAQ-devgem).

Take a look at the [official release post](http://wiki.openqa.org/display/WTR/2007/09/05/Watir+1.5.2+Released) at Watir wiki.
