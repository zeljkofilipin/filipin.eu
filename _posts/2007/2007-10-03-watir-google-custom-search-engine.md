Watir general mailing list has moved a few times and that has complicated searching of archives. I have created [Watir Google Custom Search Engine](http://www.google.com/coop/cse?cx=007267089725385613265%3Agmydx5gtw6u) that "searches all Watir related mailing lists, forums and groups (and nothing else)." At least ones that I am familiar with. To be specific:

[Watir General](http://groups.google.com/group/watir-general) at Google Groups

[Watir General](http://forums.openqa.org/forum.jspa?forumID=5) at OpenQA Forums  
[Watir Developers](http://forums.openqa.org/forum.jspa?forumID=6) at OpenQA Forums

[wtr-general](http://www.mail-archive.com/wtr-general@rubyforge.org/) at The Mail Archive

[Wtr-general](http://rubyforge.org/pipermail/wtr-general/) at RubyForge  
[Wtr-development](http://rubyforge.org/pipermail/wtr-development/) at RubyForge  
[Wtr-core](http://rubyforge.org/pipermail/wtr-core/) at RubyForge
