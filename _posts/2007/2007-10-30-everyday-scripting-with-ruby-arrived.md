Long long time ago, I was reviewer of [Brian Marick's](http://www.testing.com/) [Everyday Scripting with Ruby](http://www.pragprog.com/titles/bmsft/). On February 2, 2007 Pragmatic Programmers said they would send me a free copy, but it [did not arrive](/everyday-scripting-with-ruby-for-teams-testers-and-you-by-brian-marick/) for a long time. I exchanged a few e-mails with them, and today the book arrived. I did not even have time to unwrap it yet. :)
