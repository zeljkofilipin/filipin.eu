title: Arachno Ruby IDE Is Not Dead
---
This morning I was pleasantly surprised when I found a piece of e-mail in my inbox from Lothar Scholz, developer of [Arachno Ruby IDE](http://www.ruby-ide.com/ruby/ruby_ide_and_ruby_editor.php) saying that [Arachno is not dead](http://www.scriptolutions.com/weblog/index.php?/archives/1-Whats-going-on-with-Arachno.html). I have been using it for years, but recently I switched to [NetBeans](http://www.netbeans.org/) because Arachno really looked dead. I will give Arachno another try.
