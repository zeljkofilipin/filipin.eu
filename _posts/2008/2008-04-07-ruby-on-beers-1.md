The first Ruby on Beers meeting happened last Saturday, April 5th, 2008 at lake Jarun, Zagreb, Croatia.

Ruby on Beers is informal gathering of Ruby users. [It all started at](http://groups.google.com/group/ruby-hr/browse_thread/thread/dde9f2c93c17fd3e) [ruby-hr](http://groups.google.com/group/ruby-hr/) Google group (in Croatian) that came to life after the first [BarCamp Zagreb](https://web.archive.org/web/20090417214325/http://barcamp.ini.hr/) where we realized that there are more than one Ruby user in the near (as I thought before Barcamp).

The four of us appeared. [Branimir Šloser](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sloser), [Saša Branković](http://www.linkedin.com/in/sbrankovic), [Mislav Marohnić](https://github.com/mislav) and me.

We enjoyed excellent Tomislav beer and discussed [the meaning of life, the universe, and everything](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Answer_to_Life,_the_Universe,_and_Everything), and more specifically, what to do next.

The next meeting should be on Saturday, May 10th, 2008. I hope I will be able to talk about [Watir](http://watir.com/), and there are rumors that there will be something said about [Git](http://git-scm.com/) and [Unicode](http://unicode.org/). Only the time will tell.

If you are in the near, please drop by.
