Blogging again! :)

I had to travel a long way to get to [AWTA 2009](http://awta.wikispaces.com/AWTA+2009) in beautiful Austin, Texas.

To see what is going on, the best place is [\#awta](http://search.twitter.com/search?q=%23awta) Twitter channel.

I am [twittering](http://twitter.com/zeljkofilipin), posting images, blogging and podcasting like crazy.

At [Notes from AWTA 2009](http://awta.wikispaces.com/Notes+from+AWTA+2009) you can find a lot of images, notes, podcasts, blog posts, presentations, twitter feed...

It was great to finally meet in person a lot of people I knew on-line for years. I have learned a lot how other people test. Also, Austin and Texas were very interesting to explore. I think all is already said about AWTA 2009. I just hope I will be there the next year too.
