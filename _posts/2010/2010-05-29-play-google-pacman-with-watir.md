I found a link to [Play Pacman with Selenium 2](http://seleniumexamples.com/blog/examples/play-pacman-with-selenium-2/) blog post at [Watir General Google group](http://groups.google.com/group/watir-general/browse_thread/thread/a2e9e914c3676df9#) and I decided to try it with Watir.

The best score so far is 3150, but the best score recorded is [2280](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KzuTKQgoY3U). Dave says he got to [2660](http://seleniumexamples.com/blog/examples/play-pacman-with-selenium-2/comment-page-1/#comment-102) with Selenium.

The source is on [github.com/zeljkofilipin/pacman](http://github.com/zeljkofilipin/pacman). Feel free to fork.
