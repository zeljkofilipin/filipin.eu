I have read [Software Craftsmanship: The New Imperative](http://www.pearsonhighered.com/educator/product/Software-Craftsmanship-The-New-Imperative/9780201733860.page) by [Pete McBreen](http://www.mcbreen.ab.ca/). I liked the book and I want to write this blog post as a reminder for myself that I have read it. Unfortunately, I do not have the time to write a good blog post, so here is just one thought:

> Software engineering works on really big projects, software craftsmanship works on smaller projects.

I like the idea of moving from apprentice to journeymen to master.
