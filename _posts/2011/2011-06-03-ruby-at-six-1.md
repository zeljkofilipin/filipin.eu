Yesterday I was at the first [Ruby at Six](http://www.amiando.com/rubyatsix.html), organized by [Infinum](http://www.infinum.hr/).

About 30 people showed up. Everybody said a few words about themselves, we had a few home brew beers and talked informally.

I said a few words about the conference I am organizing, [viaqa](http://viaqa.mobi/). The first day is general software testing topics, the second day is [Watir Day](http://viaqa.mobi/category/watir-day/). Price? [Free](http://viaqa.mobi/tickets/)!

If you want to continue the conversation, go to [ruby-hr](http://groups.google.com/group/ruby-hr) Google group or [#rubyatsix](https://twitter.com/#!/search?q=%23rubyatsix) at Twitter.
