Yesterday I was at [Zagreb STC MeetUp](https://github.com/zeljkofilipin/zagreb-stc/wiki) [\#4](http://www.meetup.com/SoftwareTestingClub/events/59949652/). As always, it was organized by [Karlo Šmid](http://zagorskisoftwaretester.blogspot.com/) at [Ericsson Nikola Tesla](http://www.ericsson.com/hr/).

As usual, about ten of us appeared. We started by introductions. Everybody said a few words about themselves, and the conversations just started. And lasted for more than 3 hours. We were talking mostly about everyday challenges that we face.

We were also talking about [Five Schools of Software Testing](http://www.scribd.com/Cyril%20Wang/d/12567324-A-Close-Look-at-the-Five-Schools-of-Software-Testing), upcoming local conference on software testing [viaqa](http://viaqa.mobi/) and the talk on software testing that a few of us plan to give at [Web::Strategija 14](http://www.webstrategija.com/14) conference.

A few of us brought the books they own and our little [library](https://github.com/zeljkofilipin/zagreb-stc/wiki/Library) continues!

Of course there, were pizza and soft drinks. (Thanks Karlo.)
