Another [game](/tag/game/) from Google: [Soccer](https://www.google.com/doodles/soccer-2012). I am a day late with it, but I had a problem. The game was reacting to `space`, but not to `left` and `right`. [Justin Ko](http://stackoverflow.com/q/11898491/17469) suggested I should try with moving the mouse left and right, and it worked!

The code is pretty trivial, as usual, and at [github.com/zeljkofilipin/olympics](https://github.com/zeljkofilipin/olympics). For now the the goalie just randomly moves left, right and jumps. The best score (4 points) I got when I changed the script so the goalie does not move at all.
