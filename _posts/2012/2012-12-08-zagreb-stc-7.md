Last week I was at [Zagreb STC](https://github.com/zeljkofilipin/zagreb-stc/wiki) [MeetUp \#7](http://www.meetup.com/SoftwareTestingClub/events/88338342/). As always, it was organized by [Karlo Šmid](http://zagorskisoftwaretester.blogspot.com/) at [Ericsson Nikola Tesla](http://www.ericsson.com/hr/).

About 10 testers and/or developers attended, as usual. I was late for the meeting so I was not there for the introductions. This time we did not have any formal talks but that does not mean we were sitting there for a few hours in silence. We had more than enough topics to talk about, mostly about software development and testing.

I brought the books that I own and our little [library](https://github.com/zeljkofilipin/zagreb-stc/wiki/Library) continues! (I am still reading [Testing Computer Software](http://www.amazon.com/Testing-Computer-Software-2nd-Edition/dp/0471358460) (2nd Edition) by Cem Kaner, Jack Falk and Hung Q. Nguyen.)

We had pizza and soft drinks, this time sponsored by my little company.
