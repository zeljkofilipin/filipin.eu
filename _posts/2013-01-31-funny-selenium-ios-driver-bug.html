Funny Selenium iOS driver bug

The other day I fixed a couple of failing Selenium iOS tests. Looks like there is something wrong in the way Selenium sends special keys (like enter or shift) to iOS devices.

I made a script that sent all possible special keys to a textarea, and the results were pretty funny. It is a known bug.
