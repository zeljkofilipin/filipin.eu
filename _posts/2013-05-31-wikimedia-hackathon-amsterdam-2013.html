Windmill in Amsterdam next to Brouwerij ‘t IJ

Last week I was in Amsterdam. I have spent a few days sightseeing with my family and a few days at Wikimedia Hackathon Amsterdam 2013.

Sightseeing

We visited Artis Royal Zoo, Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh Museum and NEMO Science Centre. One day we rented bikes and biked all over the city. We also took boat sightseeing tour.

Hackathon

Chris wrote a pretty good summary of what the two of us did at the hackathon. Take a look at QA: new mail list, update from Amsterdam, etc. post at qa@lists.wikimedia.org mailing list.

Beer

I have visited a few nice beer places. If you are into beer and in Amsterdam visit Brouwerij ‘t IJ, Brouwerij de Prael and Arendsnest.
