Yesterday I was at [Zagreb STC MeetUp \#10](http://zagorskisoftwaretester.blogspot.com/2013/04/announcement-for-zagreb-stc-10-meetup.html). This is the first time the meetup was at [ZIP.Factory](http://zipzg.com/). We had the first international speaker, [Gojko Adzic](http://gojko.net/). Gojko had an hour long talk and then we had an hour or two of discussion. The talk mentioned [impact mapping](http://impactmapping.org/) and [MindMup](http://www.mindmup.com/), among other things.

Before the meetup, [Karlo](http://zagorskisoftwaretester.blogspot.com/) and I picked up Gojko at the airport and gave him a short tour around downtown Zagreb. After the meetup we drove him to Tuhelj. He had a talk at [Agile Adria](http://agileadria.com/) conference earlier today.

I am really happy to say that our small [library](https://github.com/zeljkofilipin/zagreb-stc/wiki/Library) continues. The books were returned and loaned.
