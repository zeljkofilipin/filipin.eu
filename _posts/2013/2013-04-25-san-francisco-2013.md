In late February this year I have spend a couple of weeks in San Francisco. I work remotely for [the Wikimedia Foundation](http://wikimediafoundation.org/wiki/Home) so I have been visiting the mother ship ([photo](http://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Wikimedia_Foundation_office_1.jpg)). A lot of stuff happened while I was there.

## Tech

I have atended a lot of meetings, paired with people, attended meetups... For example, I was in the office while they were recording VisualEditor video ([photo](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File%3AWikimedia_Foundation_office_3.jpg)).

<iframe src="https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File%3ASneak_Preview_-_Wikipedia_VisualEditor.webm?embedplayer=yes" width="512" height="288" frameborder="0"></iframe>

The first meetup I attended was Discussion about English Wikipedia Dispute Resolution with Steven Zhang ([photo](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Discussion_about_English_Wikipedia_Dispute_Resolution_with_Steven_Zhang_2.jpg)).

Another one was [Wikimedia & wikiHow mobile updates](http://www.meetup.com/Wikipedia-Engineering-Meetup/events/100444752/) ([photos](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Wikimedia_%26_wikiHow_mobile_updates)). [Chris McMahon](http://chrismcmahonsblog.blogspot.com/) and I had [one minute talk about browser automation](http://youtu.be/8ccTm_IdZBw?t=46m44s). We start at 46:44.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/8ccTm_IdZBw?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Chris and I had a bit longer talk about browser automation at a [Wikimedia Open Tech Chat](https://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/Meetings/2013-02-28) ([photos](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Wikimedia_Open_Tech_Chat)). We talk for the first 14 minutes.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/3UPDzPBf4t8?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

I was at [Tales From the Selenium Testing Trenches](http://www.meetup.com/seleniumsanfrancisco/events/101087592/) meetup organised by [San Francisco Selenium Meetup Group](http://www.meetup.com/seleniumsanfrancisco/) at [Lookout Mobile Security](https://www.lookout.com/) office. At the meetup I have met [R. Tyler Croy](http://www.unethicalblogger.com/), organiser of [Testing and Automation devroom](https://fosdem.org/2013/schedule/track/testing_and_automation/) at [FOSDEM 2013](https://fosdem.org/2013/). He works at Lookout Mobile Security, so he gave me a short tour around the office before the meetup.

After the meetup I had a beer or two with a few people, including Chris and [Michael Larsen](http://www.mkltesthead.com/). Michael wrote ["...and Those Automated Browser Tests Totally Saved My Ass!"](http://www.mkltesthead.com/2013/03/and-those-automated-browser-tests.html) blog post the next day.

Chris and I had dinner first with [Elisabeth Hendrickson](http://testobsessed.com/) and then with [Marlena Compton](http://marlenacompton.com/), both of [Pivotal Labs](http://pivotallabs.com/). Marlena invited us to breakfast at Pivotal Labs, so we visited their San Francisco office.

## Beer

I was sampling beer ([21st Amendment Brewery](http://21st-amendment.com/), [ThirstyBear Brewing Company](http://www.thirstybear.com/), [Magnolia Gastropub and Brewery](http://www.magnoliapub.com/), [Toronado Pub](http://www.toronado.com/), to mention a few). I was able to get a short tour of 21st Amendment Brewery ([photos](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:21st_Amendment_Brewery)). I also visited [San Francisco Brewcraft](http://www.sanfranciscobrewcraft.com/) brewshop ([photo](http://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:San_Francisco_Brewcraft.jpg)).

## Sightseeing

I also did some sightseeing. One day I walked to [Fishermans Wharf](http://www.fishermanswharf.org/) ([photo](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Fishermans_Wharf_10.jpg)). I tried getting to [Alcatraz](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alcatraz_Island) but it was sold out. This [photo](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Alcatraz_Island_11.jpg) shows the closest I was to it. I stumbled at [California sea lions at PIER 39](http://www.pier39.com/home/attractions/on-the-pier/sea-lions/) ([photo](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:California_sea_lions_at_PIER_39_1.jpg)), [Musee Mecanique](http://www.museemechanique.org/) ([photo](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Musee_Mecanique_5.jpg)) and [Chinese New Year Parade](http://www.chineseparade.com/). The [Old Cathedral of Saint Mary of the Immaculate Conception](http://www.oldsaintmarys.org/) ([photo](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:The_Old_Cathedral_of_Saint_Mary_of_the_Immaculate_Conception.jpg)) was was near my hotel, so I have visited it. Can you tell from the photo that it is in Chinatown? One day I have rented a bike and had a nice ride to the [Golden Gate Bridge](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Golden_Gate_Bridge) ([photo](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Golden_Gate_Bridge_13.jpg)) and back.
