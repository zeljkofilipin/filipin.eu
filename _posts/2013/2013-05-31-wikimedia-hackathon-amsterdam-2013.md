Last week I was in Amsterdam. I have spent a few days sightseeing with my family and a few days at [Wikimedia Hackathon Amsterdam 2013](http://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/Amsterdam_Hackathon_2013).

**Sightseeing**

We visited [Artis Royal Zoo](http://www.artis.nl/en/artis-royal-zoo/), [Rijksmuseum](https://www.rijksmuseum.nl/en), [Van Gogh Museum](http://www.vangoghmuseum.nl/vgm/index.jsp) and [NEMO Science Centre](http://www.e-nemo.nl/en/?id=5). One day we rented bikes and biked all over the city. We also took boat sightseeing tour.

**Hackathon**

Chris wrote a pretty good summary of what the two of us did at the hackathon. Take a look at [QA: new mail list, update from Amsterdam, etc.](http://lists.wikimedia.org/pipermail/qa/2013-May/000002.html) post at [qa@lists.wikimedia.org](https://lists.wikimedia.org/mailman/listinfo/qa) mailing list.

**Beer**

I have visited a few nice beer places. If you are into beer and in Amsterdam visit [Brouwerij ‘t IJ](http://www.brouwerijhetij.nl/homepage/spring-paasij/), [Brouwerij de Prael](http://deprael.nl/) and [Arendsnest](http://www.arendsnest.nl/).
