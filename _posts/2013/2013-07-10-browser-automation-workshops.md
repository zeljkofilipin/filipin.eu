[Chris McMahon](http://chrismcmahonsblog.blogspot.com/), [Quim Gil](http://flors.wordpress.com/) and I have started to organize browser automation workshops. We are trying to get more volunteers involved in [testing MediaWiki, software that runs Wikipedia](/how-mediawiki-software-that-runs-wikipedia-is-tested/). Workshops are a delicious combination of [Google+ Hangouts](https://www.google.com/+/learnmore/hangouts/), [Etherpad](http://etherpad.org/), [IRC](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internet_Relay_Chat) and code. We will teach you everything we know about browser automation with [Selenium](http://docs.seleniumhq.org/), [Ruby](http://www.ruby-lang.org/en/), [Cucumber](http://cukes.info/) and [page object pattern](https://code.google.com/p/selenium/wiki/PageObjects). For free! If you sign up today, we will even add a few more [buzzwords](http://search.dilbert.com/comic/Buzzword) to the mix!\*
